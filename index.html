Overview

This research explores two main methods for synthesizing natural-sounding code-switched speech between Mandarin and English using the FastSpeech2 model. Various techniques and alignments have been tested to optimize voice quality and accuracy in bilingual speech synthesis.

Methodology

The study is divided into several groups where each group was given different sets of data for voice synthesis experiments. Method 1 focuses on separate phoneme modeling for Mandarin and English, while Method 2 adopts a unified IPA-based approach for both languages to streamline phonological features and improve consistency across languages.
